Welcome to NaAVIC

NaAVIC is a free Electronic Chart System in your pocket that will make marine navigation easy, safe, reliable, and fun.

This free app is for recreational boaters who want to navigate the surrounding waters safely while exploring new and interesting locations to fish, dive, eat etc. The app offers a range of features to make navigation easier and reliable that are delivered through a clear and intuitive interface designed specifically for mobile devices and tablets.

By utilizing a cloud-based approach with data streaming, the NaAVIC ECS enables all members of an expedition to more actively engage in the voyage as well as to share that voyage with fellow ‘marine friends’, hence building a community of like-minded users.

Awesome Features

Marine Base Map

Supports IHO S-57 charts and S-52 presentation and provides seamless data coverage through navigation.

Route Management

Comprehensive Route Creation and Navigation. Import and Export Waypoints into RTZ or KML formats.

Offline Navigation

Download Maps into Device
and Navigate Offline when
mobile network not available.

North up or Head up Display Orientation

Orientation of Charts for North up and Head up Display.

Route Planning and Management Tools

Create Way Points and plan routes
to navigate between them. Edit,
Clear and Redirect Waypoints as wish

Displays Position, Speed and
Course

Displays boat Course on Ground, Speed on Ground when GPS activated. Display real time instruments including speed, course, bearing etc.

Location and Feature Query Tools

Display Ship location on
screen and Query tool
provides more details on features

Mobile GPS Integration

Plot your position in real time using your device with GPS Integration and track positioning, tracks history and waypoints.

Point of Interest

Find recreational hotspots (diving, fishing, surfing etc.) based on other NaAVIC user reporting/ NaAVIC community reporting.

Day and Night Color Palettes

Visualize data in Day & Night color modes avoid user’s eyes needing to readjust to a difference light intensity.

Notifications & Alarms

Be notified with audio for registered users sharing information. Get Alarm for dangerous depth approach and notification display on mobile.

Real-time Route Monitoring

Monitoring of Waypoint, Course,
Distances and Cross track deviation
from preset limits in real-time

Data Layers

Streaming additional data layers over base
map like high resolution bathymetry,
imagery, hazards, aids, facilities etc.

Measure Distances

Measure distance and bearing
between vessel and known point
or between two known points.
